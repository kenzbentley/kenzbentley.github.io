Keep Austin Genuine

This month we’re highlighting Genuine Joe Coffee House which has been in business for well over a decade. The place is known for its homey vibe and friendly staff, housed in a wonderfully renovated 100 year old farmhouse. Unfortunately, it will be closing its doors next month at the end of May.

The landowner that Genuine Joe's leases the house from was approached by an investor and received an offer he couldn’t refuse. A high rise condo will be built in the beloved coffee shop’s place. At the moment the owner and staff of Genuine Joes are figuring out where to go next.

On May 13th, they’ll be having a going away party featuring live music, art booths, and a double feature documentary about the unique place. Check them out before they go and donate to their relocation fund.

“We’re going to keep fighting and try to relocate”
