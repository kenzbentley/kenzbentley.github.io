Skypia, island in the clouds

For the past two years I been on a search for the mysterious sky island above the clouds of the bermuda triangle. The journey began on da winter of 2019. We met a man whose ancestor saw gold on an island. But when returning, the island had disappeared.

Our new friend Marty been diving every day for the past 20 years he did, in search of the gold seen by his ancestor on the hunch that the island sank. He found but 3 pieces of the gold. We heard of a story, pirates catching an updraft that sends their ship in the sky.

I won’t spoil anything cause we’ve been missing for 2 years, and return with the gold. While unbelievable it might seem, we found the island and the treasure. Stay tuned as I’ll be updating you on our 2 year adventure to finding skypia, the island in the sky.
